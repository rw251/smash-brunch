      SMASH

      Welcome to SMASH, the medication safety dashboard!

      This system examines electronic health records and identifies patients who may be potentially at risk due to the medications that they are receiving. These lists of patients are then displayed to support health professionals when making decisions about patient care which could lead to improvements in medication safety in primary care.

      The system is currently being evaluated in Salford Clinical Commissioning Group (CCG), with general practices receiving pharmacist support to help address medication safety challenges highlighted by the dashboard. User feedback will be incorporated into a second version of the dashboard for wider rollout across Greater Manchester.

      If you would like more information, please contact support@smash.srft.nhs.uk.

      This dashboard has been developed by the NIHR Greater Manchester Patient Safety Translational Research Centre (GM PSTRC).
